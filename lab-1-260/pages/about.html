About the Photographer



Hello! My name is Ellie Van De Graaff Vail. I have been interested in photography
since the first time my family visited Europe. I was so intrigued how beautiful
everything was the entire time we were there, and I wanted to be able to capture the beauty.
I got into photography upon returning home, and was of course ecstatic when I was
able to return to Europe. This collection of pictures is from the second
time I visited with my family. I love traveling, seeing new places, trying lots of new
food, and of course, taking pictures of it all.

When I'm not taking pictures, I love to spend time with
my husband (pictured left), Tyler. We got married in September and
couldn't be happier! I love reading, sleeping (what college student doesn't?),
boating, playing volleyball and piano, and going to visit my
family as often as possible. Thank you so much for
visiting my page, and please feel free to contact me with any questions.
